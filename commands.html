New Commands

$website - Get the url of my website

$randomNum - Gets a random number from 9999999999 to 0

$dox [player] - Find someones "ip"

Commands

$ping [player] - Shows you or other player's ping

$bestping - Gets the player's best ping.

$worstping - Gets the player's worst ping.

$coords - Gets the coordinates of the bot.

$follow - Lets the bot follow you (must be in range).

$stop - Lets the bot stop following you.

$radius - Gets the player that is in range

$kill - Kills the bot.

$urban <query> - Looks up the definition in Urban Dictionary.

$whisper <username> <message...> - Sends a whisper to a player (must be an online player to do this).

$prioq - Gets the priority queue from 2b2t.

$q(ue)(ue) - Gets the queue (only works on 9b9t).

$2bq(ue)(ue) - Gets the queue from 2b2t.

$f - Pays for respects

$goto [y] or $goto [x] [z] or $goto [x] [y] [z] - Goes to the specified loccation.

$come - Makes the bot come to you. (Must be in range to do this)

$2bseen [username] - Looks up the username that was last seen on 2b2t.

$2bstats [username] - Gets the stats on 2b2t.

$2bkill [username] - Gets the kills of a player from 2b2t.

$2bdeath [username] - Gets deaths of a player from 2b2t.

$2bstatus [username] - Gets the status of a player from 2b2t.

$health - Gets the bot's health.

$hunger - Get's the bot's hunger and saturation.

$penis - Gets your penis size.

$howgayis [name] - Shows how gay you are or others.

$howgayare [name] - Similar to $howgay, shows how gay are them.

$ratewaifu [name] - Lets waifu rate you or others.

$yes - yes.

$no - no.

$[first]seen [username] - Gets a player of when a player joined the server.

$[last]seen [username] - Gets a player who last joined on the server.

$firstmessage/firstwords - Gets a player's first words.

$lastmessage/lastwords - Gets a player's last words

$jd/joindate - similar to -firstseen

$messages [player] - Counts how many messages you or other players have sent

$joins [player] - Counts how many times you or other players have joined

$leaves [player] - Counts how many times you or other players have left

$quote [player] - Gets a player's quote

$r/reddit <subreddit> - Gets the new subreddit post, for example: $reddit r/2b2t, $r 2b2t

$discord - Official PistonCubeBOT Discord

$players - Gets the server's players.

$oldnames <player> - Gets the player's last names (player must have a known account)

$dupe - Dupes an item.

$kit <name> - Gives you a kit.

$give <name> - Gives you an item with that name

$gamemode <mode> Switches your game mode

$op - Gives yourself op

$tps [server] - Gets the TPS of the server.

$ip [server] - Gets the IP address of the server.
